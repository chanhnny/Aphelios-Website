Free Aphelios Guide Diamond+

Role

As a marksman, Aphelios dishes out long range attacks at high speed. He excels at damaage per second, but must position himself to safety in order to do so.

Difficulty

Aphelios is one of the hardest champions in the game to date. With five unique weapons to work with and a variety of combos, it can be hard to keep up with what to use when to use it and when to get rid of it. He is also a high target for enemy teams, so proper mechanics of an ADC are required.

Synergy

Because of Aphelios' high damage output, he doesn't need a kill heavy support. Instead, an enchanter is his best partner 99% of the time. With a heals, shields, and plenty of cc, he will less problems dying to the enemy team and can focus on attacking.
